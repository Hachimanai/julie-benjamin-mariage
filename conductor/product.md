# Initial Concept
A digital wedding invitation for Julie & Benjamin at Domaine de la Rainville on July 24, 2027.

# Product Guide

## Vision
A luxurious, elegant, and highly responsive digital wedding invitation that provides guests with essential details about the event, and allows them to manage their presence (RSVP) easily. The aesthetic follows the "Revelia Studio" style, with a minimalist, clean, and parchment-inspired color palette.

## Target Audience
Friends and family of Julie and Benjamin who are invited to the wedding celebration.

## Key Features
- **Event Details:** Presentation of the date (Samedi 24 juillet 2027) and location (Domaine de la Rainville, Villampuy).
- **Countdown Timer:** A dynamic countdown to the wedding day.
- **RSVP Management:** Interactive buttons to confirm or decline attendance, designed seamlessly into the UI without standard radio inputs.
- **Responsive Layout:** A mobile-first design centered within a max-width of 500px for optimal readability and elegant framing.
