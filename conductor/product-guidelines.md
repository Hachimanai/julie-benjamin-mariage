# Product Guidelines

## Visual Identity
- **Primary Color:** Royal Blue (`#4169E1`) for accents, icons, and buttons.
- **Background Colors:** Light Parchment Beige (`#faf7f2`) for inner sections, Neutral Beige (`#f2eee9`) for global background.
- **Typography:** 
  - **Inter:** Standard body text, labels, and buttons.
  - **Cormorant Garamond:** Section titles and dates.
  - **Great Vibes:** Cursive for the names of the couple.

## Layout & UX
- **Structure:** Central column layout with a maximum width of `500px`.
- **Spacing:** Vertical padding of `40px` between sections (total `80px` inter-section spacing).
- **Components:** Sub-sections must use a `1rem` border radius and the `.inner-beige` class.
- **Interactivity:** Button-based presence management without visible radio inputs. Smooth transitions and clear interactive feedback.

## Prose & Content
- **Tone:** Elegant, formal yet warm, and minimalist.
- **Language:** French (as per the target names and location).
- **Clarity:** Information should be presented cleanly with clear visual hierarchy.
